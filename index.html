Prepositions of Time

....

At

at a point in time:

at one o'clock, half-past five, midnight;

at lunchtime, dinner-time;

at the end of the day.

On

on a day or date:

on Tuesday, Thursday;

on 1 April, 4th June;

on the first day of spring.

In

in a period of time:

in the morning, afternoon, evening;

in April, May, June;

in the summer, winter;

in 1992.

Important Notes!

on Wednesday, in the afternoon, on Wednesday afternoon.

in the morning, afternoon, evening, but at night.

on Christmas Eve, Independance Day, Easter Sunday, but at Christmas.
